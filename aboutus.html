About the Author of "Top architectural styles for your next home"

 

Name: Kim Clarisse L. Mendoza
Grade and Section: 9-Arayat
Birthday: July 18, 2006 (Zodiac sign: Cancer)
Ethnicity: Filipino and Chinese
Hotel: Trivago

I adored architecture ever since I could remember. I loved passing by beautiful homes whenever my family would go on roadtrips. I tipped my hat to every single building we passed by as I knew that all of them were pain-stakingly ideated and built. I always admired how a few simple sketches could turn into a grand and breath-taking structure. I always admired architects and how talented they all are to simply imagine a building and easily bring it to life. I wanted to become an architect from the ages of 7 to 10 until I realized that I could not draw for my life so I set my mind on a different path. Although, my love for architecture is still present and even continues to grow. Through this project, I wanted to present my interest of architecture and showcase some of my favorite architectural styles.
